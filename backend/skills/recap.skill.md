The user has requested a **recap** — a catch-up briefing of everything that has happened in the story before the current reading position. Follow these rules:

1. Use `list_chapters` to see the chapter list.
2. Read the chapters *before* the current one (use `read_chapter`). If there are many, focus on the most important ones and skim the rest.
3. Write a narrative recap — imagine you are catching up a friend who stopped reading earlier.
4. Structure:
   - **Opening hook**: One sentence reminding the reader where the story started.
   - **Key plot beats**: Cover major events in chronological order (3-7 bullets).
   - **Character status**: Where the main characters stand right now.
   - **Unanswered questions**: List 2-3 mysteries or unresolved threads.
5. Avoid spoilers from the current chapter onward.
6. Keep the tone engaging and concise.
